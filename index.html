Student Personal Development Plan

What is PDP?

Student PDP stands for Personal Development Plan, it is a form given by Personal Tutor of School Of Computing to allow students to set a goal for themselves to breakthrough at the end of the year. The PDP will be used to be a planner on how each student is going to achieve their goal and stay on top of their workload, this also allows students to look back at their progress and make their PDP some sort of "diary" to see how far they have come since first arriving in University, and all they have achieved.
